Activities

Monthly Meetups

Meetups with speakers held at sponsors premises. People can submit their speks beforehand or have quick lightning talks with short notice. These meetups have limited available seats and the admission is free.

Pub Nights

Public, informal and casual meetups where everybody can join and discuss freely. These are good events to attend if you want to have a beer and mingle with new people.

Hackathons

There has been interest for some sort of hackathons.

ClojuTRE

Yearly event in Tampere organized by Metosin. ClojuTRE web site

Clojure Cup

Globally distributed programming competition for Clojure and ClojureScript programmers organized by @teropa. Clojure Cup web site

ClojureBridge

www.ClojureBridge.org: "ClojureBridge aims to increase diversity within the Clojure community by offering free, beginner-friendly Clojure programming workshops for women."

See this google groups thread if you want to help bringing ClojureBridge to Finland.
